About us

Upload photos to ummi.io from any device, sort them using the power of cloud vision, then download the organized albums back to your device, all in a matter of seconds.

Sort photos by...

  • Content (dogs, cats, screenshots...)
  • Emotions (happy, sad, suprised...)
  • Landmarks (statue of liberty, grand canyon...)
  • Explicit content


    • How to use UMMI

    1. Upload

      2. Upload multiple images from your device, or take photos directly from your device's camera

    2. Sort

      3. Select one of the sorting albums, wait for the response from google vision

    3. Create

      4. Create the albums you want by clicking on the tags, emotions, or landmarks found

    4. Download

      5. Click the Save Album button on any album you want to download back to your device.
